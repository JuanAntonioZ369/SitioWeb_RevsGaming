Everything you need to play retro

No emulator setup. No BIOS hunting. Straight to the game.

Auto Game Library
Point to any folder with your games and RevsGaming detects, organizes and displays them with cover art. List or grid view with instant search.
PS1 Netplay — 2 Players
Create a room and get a 6-letter code. Your friend enters it and the game launches automatically. Powered by Mednafen, the most accurate PS1 emulator.
Netplay up to 4 Players
Using RetroArch and our own MITM server in Lima, Peru, you can play with up to 4 friends on most classic systems.
Friends System
See who is online, what game they are playing, and invite them directly to your room. All in real time thanks to Supabase Realtime.
Cloud Saves
Your save files sync automatically to the cloud via Supabase Storage. Never lose your progress even if you reformat your PC.
Controller, Guest & Auto BIOS
Navigate the whole app with your controller. Play without an account in guest mode with a nickname. PS1 BIOS downloads automatically on install.
